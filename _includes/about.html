About Me

A brief look into my personal experiences.

  • My Roots

    Origins of my Passion

    Born in Shanghai, raised in the bay area, Los Angeles Lakers fan. Interesting how that worked out. Ever since I was a child, I have been surrounded by technology, from nearby tech giants Google and Apple, to my own parents who worked in the technology sector. Technology has been part of my life from the very beginning and my passion for it continues to grow.

  • Dartmouth College

    Class of 2017

    In September of 2014, I decided to attend Dartmouth College, a small liberal arts college in Hanover, New Hampshire. Although Dartmouth was an enormous change from the suburban life I have grown used to, the college has grown on me and allowed me to appreciate where I came from even more. At Dartmouth I am a computer science major and mathematics minor.

  • Waterfall

    Create mobile mmarketing customers love.

    During the summer of 2014, I was a member of the Waterfall team, a mobile marketing startup based in San Francisco. At Waterfall, I worked as a software engineering intern for the Special Projects team. Later after the summer, I worked remotely continuing my internship as part of the Web Services team.

  • DALI Lab

    Digital Arts, Leadership, & Innovation Lab in Computer Science

    Starting my Sophomore year, I joined the DALI Lab at Dartmouth College as a software developer. The DALI Lab is a research and development lab where students work together to build prototypes for real world partners.
